About MamaCare AI

MamaCare AI is dedicated to providing comprehensive maternal health support through advanced artificial intelligence. Our platform offers 24/7 guidance, monitoring, and emergency detection for expectant mothers, especially in rural and underserved areas.

Our Mission

To reduce maternal mortality rates and improve pregnancy outcomes by making quality healthcare accessible to all expectant mothers through AI-powered technology.

Pregnancy Tracking

Our comprehensive pregnancy tracking system helps you monitor your journey from conception to delivery:

  • Weekly Progress Updates: Track your baby's development week by week
  • Symptom Logging: Record and monitor pregnancy symptoms
  • Appointment Reminders: Never miss important prenatal appointments
  • Nutrition Guidance: Personalized dietary recommendations
  • Exercise Plans: Safe workout routines for each trimester

Risk Detection

Our AI-powered risk detection system provides early warning for potential complications:

  • Preeclampsia Detection: Monitor blood pressure and protein levels
  • Gestational Diabetes Screening: Track glucose levels and risk factors
  • Preterm Labor Alerts: Identify early signs of premature labor
  • Fetal Movement Monitoring: Track baby's activity patterns
  • Emergency Protocols: Immediate alerts for critical situations
